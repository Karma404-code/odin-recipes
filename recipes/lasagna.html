Recipe for Lasagna

Description

A short and simple recipe for a meaty, cheesy and delicious lasagna.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion
  • Tomatoes
  • Fresh herbs
  • Sugar
  • Noodles
  • Cheese
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Cook the meat
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the cheese layer
  4. Assemble the lasagna
  5. Bake the lasagna
