Recipe for Pizza

Description

It Pizza. What more do you want?

Ingredients

  • 1 and 1/3 cups warm water (320ml)
  • 2 and 1/4 teaspoons Platinum Yeast from Red Star instant yeast (1 standard packet)
  • 1 Tablespoon granulated sugar
  • 2 Tablespoons olive oil, plus more for pan and brushing on dough
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 3 and 1/2 cups unbleached all-purpose flour (about 450g), plus more for hands and surface
  • Sprinkle of cornmeal for dusting the pan

Steps

  1. Create the pizza dough
  2. Knead and proof
  3. Form the dough
  4. Assemble and bake
